Rohit Sharma

Introduction

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time. He is a right-handed opening batsman and has played for the Indian national cricket team since 2007. Rohit Sharma is also the vice-captain of the Indian cricket team in limited-overs cricket.

Early Life

Rohit Sharma was born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India. His father worked as a caretaker of a transport firm storehouse. Rohit Sharma started playing cricket at a young age and was coached by Dinesh Lad, who also coached Sachin Tendulkar.

Career

Rohit Sharma made his international debut for the Indian cricket team in June 2007. He initially struggled to establish himself in the team but eventually found success as an opening batsman in limited-overs cricket. Rohit Sharma has scored over 10,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs) and is the only player to score three double centuries in the format. He has also scored over 2,700 runs in Twenty20 Internationals (T20Is) and is the leading run-scorer in the format.

Awards and Achievements

  • Arjuna Award (2015)
  • Wisden Leading Cricketer in the World (2019)
  • ICC ODI Cricketer of the Year (2019)
  • Padma Shri (2020)
  • 5 Times IPL Champion
